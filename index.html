Frequently Asked Questions

Who needs to comply with PSTA?

Any software used on American soil, whether deployed on servers, installed on devices, or accessed via cloud services. This includes commercial software, open-source tools, SaaS platforms, mobile apps, and enterprise systems.

How much does certification cost?

American companies: $5,000 base fee + testing costs. Foreign companies: $50,000 Patriotic Certification Tax + testing costs. Annual renewal required.

What happens if my software fails testing?

Failed software receives a detailed remediation report. You have 90 days to fix issues and resubmit. Software that fails twice is placed on the ANSL watchlist.

Can I use open-source frameworks?

Yes, but only those approved by the Free and Open Source Committee (FOSC). Check the official whitelist at fosc.ansl.gov before implementation.

How long does certification take?

Standard certification: 6-12 weeks. Expedited service (additional fee): 3-4 weeks. Complex systems may require extended testing periods.
